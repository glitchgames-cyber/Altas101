Past

Pioneering institutions like TIFR and ISRO laid the groundwork for computing, space research, and electronics manufacturing, while IT services began positioning India as the world’s back-office.

Public sector labs, IITs, and DoE task forces nurtured component design, language computing, and telecom policies long before venture capital reached the ecosystem. The cooperative model of sharing mainframe time and open academic exchanges defined this era’s collaborative spirit.

  • 1950s: First indigenous analog computers
  • 1970s: Department of Electronics formed
  • 1980s: Software export zones emerge

Present

India hosts over 100 unicorns, a thriving developer ecosystem, and massive digital public infrastructure powering finance, health, and education.

Startup hubs stretch from Bengaluru to Jaipur, SaaS exports cross $20B, and DPI rails like ONDC and OCEN bring kirana stores, farmers, and MSMEs into the digital mainstream. Global chip giants partner with local fabs as semicon talent pipelines expand.

  • UPI crossing 10B+ monthly transactions
  • Semiconductor and space startups accelerating
  • India Stack inspiring global policy

Future

Deep-tech, quantum computing, green engineering, and inclusive AI will shape the next leaps as India aspires to become a product powerhouse.

National missions envision moon bases, ocean tech corridors, and gigafactories with circular supply chains. Bharat-scale AI copilots, high-speed 6G grids, and quantum secure networks are expected to redefine productivity for the next billion.

  • Chandrayaan & Gaganyaan inspire space-tech
  • National Quantum Mission investments
  • AI for Bharat-scale languages

Semiconductor Stack

From DLI incentives to OSAT investments in Gujarat, India is stitching together design, fabrication, and packaging capacity with a focus on 28nm and specialty nodes.

  • Micron’s assembly plant underway
  • SemiconIndia incubations for fabless teams
  • Dedicated RISC-V talent pipelines

Climate-Tech S-Curve

Green hydrogen corridors, battery intelligence startups, and mobility-as-a-service pilots unlock new unit economics for decarbonization.

  • Cascade batteries + swapping networks
  • Agri-carbon MRV platforms
  • Blue economy drones + analytics

Public Digital Infrastructure

India Stack 2.0 (Aadhaar, UPI, ONDC, OCEN, ABDM) is evolving into modular rails that other nations reference for inclusive growth.

  • Account Aggregator and OCEN for credit
  • ONDC retail and logistics unbundling
  • ABDM health data exchanges at scale

Space & Defence Startups

Skyroot, Agnikul, Bellatrix, and Pixel accelerate private missions while DRDO and ISRO open facilities for testing and shared infrastructure.

  • Reusable launch vehicle experiments
  • Hyperspectral imaging constellations
  • Dual-use sensors + avionics supply chains
