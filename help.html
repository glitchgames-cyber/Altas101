Help & FAQ

Find answers to common questions and learn how to make the most of India Tech Atlas features.

Getting Started

How do I navigate the website?

Use the navigation menu at the top to access different sections:

  • Atlas Home: Main landing page with overview
  • Past Hub: Historical tech milestones
  • Present Pulse: Current tech ecosystem
  • Future Forge: Future tech scenarios
  • Games Lab: Interactive games and challenges
  • Student Logbook: Track your progress
How do I play the games?

Each game has its own page with instructions. Visit the Games Lab to see all available games. Most games work offline and save your progress automatically using localStorage.

Can I use this offline?

Yes! India Tech Atlas is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works offline. Once you visit the site, it's cached for offline use. Your progress and data are stored locally in your browser.

Features

What is the Student Logbook?

The Student Logbook allows you to track your learning progress, save notes, and manage your journey through India Tech Atlas. All data is stored locally in your browser for privacy.

How does the Vault work?

The Vault is a secure storage area protected by a password. You can store important notes, insights, and personal data. The password is hashed using SHA-256 for security.

Can I export my data?

Yes! You can export your logbook entries and statistics. Visit the Statistics page to export your data as JSON. The Vault also supports data export functionality.

How do I search for content?

Use the Search page to find content across all pages. You can search by keywords, topics, or page names. The search is instant and works offline.

Technical

What browsers are supported?

India Tech Atlas works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. For the best experience, use the latest version of your browser.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is required! All data is stored locally in your browser. This means your data stays private and you don't need to sign up or log in.

Is my data secure?

Yes! All data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. The Vault uses password hashing for additional security. No data is sent to external servers unless you explicitly use backend features.

How do I clear my data?

You can clear data from the Statistics page or by clearing your browser's localStorage. Go to your browser settings and clear site data for this website.

Games

How do I play the 3D Agriculture Farm?

Click on the canvas to lock your mouse, then use WASD to move, mouse to look around, left-click to place blocks, right-click to remove blocks, and E to harvest crops. Plant seeds on tilled soil and wait for them to grow!

Are game scores saved?

Yes! Your game scores and progress are automatically saved to localStorage. You can view your statistics on the Statistics page.

Can I reset a game?

Most games have a reset or new game button. You can also clear your browser's localStorage to reset all game data.
