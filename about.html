About India Tech Atlas

An interactive educational platform exploring India's technology ecosystem across past, present, and future eras. Learn, play, and discover the innovations shaping India's digital transformation.

Our Mission

Education

Make India's tech history, present achievements, and future potential accessible to students, educators, and curious minds through interactive experiences and engaging content.

Innovation

Celebrate the pioneers, institutions, and startups that have shaped and continue to shape India's position as a global technology leader.

Engagement

Combine learning with play through interactive games, quizzes, and immersive experiences that make tech history come alive.
